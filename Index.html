Pharmacist System

Blood Bank System

The system is for Blood Donating companies. It is used by the Admin to register new Donors, update the blood stock which they have and delete donators who no-longer exist. Admin can check the blood types that are available in their inventory.

Patient-Doctor System

The system is for Doctors who own private surgery complexes. It is used by the patients to create accounts and set appointments with the doctor if they may need medical help.
The doctor gets to keep track of his/her appointments and can distribute medical prescriptions on the system.
